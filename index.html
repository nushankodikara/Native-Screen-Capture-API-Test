Instructions for Using Screen Recorder


To use this screen recorder, simply press the "Record" button to start recording your screen and microphone. When you're finished, press the "Stop" button to stop the recording. The recorded video will be displayed in the video player on the page.


To download the recorded video, click on the three-dot menu in the top right corner of the video player and select "Download". The video will be downloaded to your device for offline viewing.
